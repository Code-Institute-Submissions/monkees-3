about the monkees

The Monkees are an American rock and pop band formed in LA in 1965 for a television series with the same name.
The Monkees show ended in 1968, but the band kept recording music until 1971, where they split.
After a resurgence in the shows popularity in 1986, the band reuinted for a few tours, and have been sporadically touring ever since.

The Albums

click the album links to listen on Spotify.

The Monkees first self-titled album The Monkees was released on October 10th, 1966. It peaked at #1 on the US, UK and Canadian album charts, and went 5x platinum.
Their second album, More of the Monkees, released in 1967, also topped the US, UK and Canadian charts, as well as also going 5x platinum.
The final two albums of 1967, Headquaters and Pisces, Aquarius, Capricorn & Jones Ltd both went 2x platinum, as well as placing at #1 in the US album charts.
The Monkees started 1968 by releasing The Birds, The Bees & The Monkees, which ended up being their last album to have major success,
going 1x platinum and place at #3 and #6 on the US and Canadian charts respectively.
